Welcome

Brevig Editing is the home of Megan Brevig. I am writing coach, editor, and writer, who works with individuals to support their academic, creative, and professional writing endeavors.

I thrive in listening to my clients' visions and supporting them in the process of bringing those visions to life. My main goal is always to provide my clients with the confidence and the tools to get their ideas into the format to which they aspire. Throughout my career, this practice has taken many different forms: it can be as simple as being a proofreader ensuring clarity and flow, or as involved as ghostwriting a clients' autobiography from a series of notes and interviews.

After graduating with an English Literature Degree from the University of Edinburgh, I got my start by helping ESL college students perfect their theses in Berlin. This was followed by opportunities in corporate translation proofreading and then as a writing assistant for a novel.

More recently, I have been working as a writing coach for college students struggling with dyslexia and an English Literature teacher for an accelerated program that supports foreign students applying for US colleges. [ADD MORE ABOUT EDITING TRANSLATED DOCUMENTS FOR STYLE.]
